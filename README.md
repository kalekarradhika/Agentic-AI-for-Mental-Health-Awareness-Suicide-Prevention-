Agentic AI for Mental Health Awareness & Suicide Prevention
Overview

Agentic AI for Mental Health Awareness & Suicide Prevention is an AI-powered platform designed to promote mental well-being, provide personalized emotional support, and facilitate early intervention through intelligent multi-agent collaboration. Built on IBM Cloud and powered by IBM watsonx Orchestrate, the solution leverages specialized AI agents to deliver mental health awareness, emotional analysis, wellness recommendations, risk assessment, and crisis support in a safe, ethical, and scalable manner.

The platform aims to reduce stigma around mental health, improve access to trusted resources, encourage proactive self-care, and support users in identifying and addressing emotional distress before it escalates into a crisis.

Problem Statement

Mental health challenges such as stress, anxiety, depression, loneliness, and emotional distress are becoming increasingly prevalent across all age groups. Many individuals do not seek help due to social stigma, lack of awareness, limited access to professional support, or inability to recognize early warning signs. Delayed intervention can lead to worsening mental health conditions and, in severe situations, increase the risk of self-harm or suicide.

This project addresses these challenges by providing an AI-powered digital companion that promotes awareness, detects emotional distress, offers personalized support, and connects users with appropriate resources when needed.

Objectives
Promote mental health awareness and education.
Detect signs of emotional distress through conversational analysis.
Provide personalized wellness recommendations.
Encourage positive coping mechanisms and self-care practices.
Support early intervention and crisis prevention.
